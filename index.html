What is Tech.LA Fellows?

Tech.LA Fellow’s 10-week Summer Fellowship gives highly motivated students in design, engineering, and business a paid internship with a Los Angeles startup.

The Tech.LA Mindset

During the summer, Tech.LA Fellows intern with partner companies to build and improve products in engineering, design, or business positions. We believe that with all of its unique offerings—entertainment capital of the world, a cultural boon, bustling downtown, and a tech community on the rise—Los Angeles deserves a technology ecosystem as robust as what you can find up north.

Build

a family of talented, driven individuals who are proud of their work.

Expose

students to the unique qualities of Los Angeles' startup ecosystem.

Impact
